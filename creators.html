Be a part of Plantry

We collaborate with world-class creators to bring their recipes to our unique platform and put it the hands of thousands of users.

Plantry is reimagining the process of meal planning, grocery shopping and cooking. Modern touch-screen devices offer unprecedented advantages for cooking, and we're only beginning to explore the potential.

A typical collaboration works like this:

  • We help you make a weekly plan consisting of seven recipes. The plan is presented in Plantry with your logotype.
  • We localize your recipes and convert measurements.
  • Anytime your recipes are shown in the app we highlight you as the author with a link to your site or app.
  • We help each other promote the collaboration through our respective channels.

We've also helped develop entire apps such as Vegourmet, Very Veggie and Swedish Vegan. This is a significantly larger effort where work together for an ongoing basis and split app revenue fifty-fifty.
